About Our School

Where Learning
Meets Joy

Little Florets School is a premier early childhood education institution located in the heart of KPHB, Hyderabad. We are committed to providing a world-class educational experience that fosters holistic development in every child.

Our innovative approach blends the best of NEP 2020 guidelines with Montessori principles, activity-based learning, and cutting-edge technologies like AI, AR/VR to create future-ready learners from the very beginning.

Our Classes

Programs Tailored For Every Age

From playgroup to primary — each stage is designed to build foundational skills with love and expertise.

🍼 2.5+ Years

Play Group

A nurturing first-step into school life. Children explore, play, and build social bonds in a safe and warm environment.

🌟 3+ Years

Nursery / Pre-KG

Structured play-based learning introducing phonics, numbers, and creative activities to spark curiosity and confidence.

🎒 4+ Years

PP I / LKG

Language development, number sense, and social skills — all delivered through engaging, hands-on activities and stories.

✏️ 5+ Years

PP II / UKG

Pre-reading, writing readiness, and mathematical thinking — building a strong foundation for formal education ahead.

📖 6+ Years

Class 1

Formal literacy and numeracy in an engaging environment, aligned with NEP 2020 experiential learning frameworks.

🔬 7+ Years

Class 2

Deepening comprehension, problem-solving, and scientific thinking through project-based, collaborative learning.

🚀 8+ Years

Class 3

Critical thinking, communication excellence, and creative expression — preparing students for the world ahead.
